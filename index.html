Researcher

My work sits at the intersection of stellar astrophysics and data-driven computation. I study stellar populations and open clusters, focusing on dynamical evolution and star-formation histories using large surveys (Gaia DR3).

  • Gaia DR3 Data Analysis
  • Probabilistic Membership Inference
  • Stellar Kinematics

Developer

Translating complex mathematics into efficient, scalable code. I develop and apply machine learning and probabilistic techniques for scalable astro-data analysis.

  • Python (NumPy, SciPy, PyTorch)

  • Scientific Computing
