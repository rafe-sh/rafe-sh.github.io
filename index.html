Research Interests

My work sits at the intersection of stellar astrophysics and data-driven computation. I study stellar populations and open clusters, focusing on dynamical evolution and star-formation histories using large surveys like Gaia DR3. I develop and apply machine learning and probabilistic techniques for scalable astro-data analysis.
